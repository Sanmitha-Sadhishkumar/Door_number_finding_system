About:

Welcome to our innovative solution designed to address a common challenge faced by delivery professionals - the difficulty in locating specific addresses due to the absence of visible house numbers. Our project aims to simplify the identification process by providing a visual snapshot of houses based on their addresses, utilizing resources of Google Street View images. We offer an accurate representation of houses. Sometimes, the houses might not have a door number; in these cases, our app displays the road image where the house is located. Powered by Python, our project utilizes the Street View Static API and Geocoding API to ensure precise results. Our mission is to simplify the lives of both delivery professionals and recipients, ensuring that deliveries are made efficiently.





Aim of Easy Locator:


  • To provide easy and effective location of a particular address
  • To bridge the barrier gap between the common people and delivery agents
  • To avoid the confusions in locating a location
  • To provide an interactive and user-friendly user experience
  • To avoid unnecessary calls between the customer and the delivery agent
